## How Can Calcium Hydroxide be Made from Limestone
When calcium carbonate (CaCO₃) is heated, it can produce quicklime(CaO) and carbonate dioxide (CO₂). And the carbonate dioxide is driven off by air flow in lime kiln. Then when the quicklime is added with appropriate amount of water, the process can produce calcium hydroxide and heat. So this is how calcium hydroxide is made from limestone.
